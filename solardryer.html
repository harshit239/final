Technical Features

Every detail in the Solar Dryer is engineered for performance, hygiene and long-term reliability.

⚡

Solar Panels

High-efficiency PV panels power fans and digital controls with near-zero fuel cost in sunlight conditions.

💨

Solar Fans

Uniform airflow throughout the drying chamber ensures consistent, faster drying across all trays.

🏭

SS Drying Chamber

Food-grade stainless steel chamber with insulation maintains precise temperature zones throughout drying.

🔲

Stainless Trays

SS mesh trays for hygienic drying — fully removable and easy to clean between product batches.

🌡️

Digital PID Control

Precise digital PID controller with humidity sensors for accurate, repeatable drying conditions.

🔄

Auto Airflow Control

Automatic airflow management with optional IoT monitoring for remote operation and data logging.

🔩

Heavy-Duty Frame

Robust stainless steel structure with optional mobility wheels for flexible placement anywhere on-site.

☀️

Hybrid Backup

Electric and biomass backup heating ensures continuous drying operations even on overcast days.
